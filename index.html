Questions, Answered

What owners ask before signing.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use this?+
No. We handle the entire build. You get a short training video, a dashboard, and a human to call when you need us. Most clients never touch the back end.
Will the AI sound robotic or turn customers off?+
Today’s voice AI is nearly indistinguishable from a human for short, focused calls. We train the agent on your scripts, tone, and services so it sounds like your front desk, not a call center.
What if it handles a call wrong?+
Every call is recorded, transcribed, and reviewed by us during your first 30 to 90 days. We flag anything off and retrain on the spot. You keep a one-click override to route to a human.
Does it work with my current phone system and CRM?+
Almost certainly yes. We integrate with Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, Dentrix, Clio, OpenDental, Shopify, Square, Toast, and 2,000+ other tools via Zapier and Make.
How is this different from hiring a virtual receptionist?+
A virtual receptionist costs $800 to $2,500 per month, takes breaks, and can’t follow up on leads or process documents. Our AI answers every call 24/7, handles thousands of conversations simultaneously, and runs the rest of your back office at the same time.
What happens if I cancel?+
Cancel the Care Plan any time with 30 days’ notice. The automations you paid to build are yours. We hand over the accounts, workflows, and data. No hostages.
Do you serve my area?+
Yes. We serve Orange County, Los Angeles, Riverside / Inland Empire, and San Diego in person. Remote clients nationwide welcome.
